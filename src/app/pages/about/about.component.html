About Our Game

Welcome to GlobeTreck, the ultimate geography challenge where every guess takes you on a journey around the world!

GlobeTreck is an interactive web game that drops you into a random location on Earth using street view imagery. Your challenge? Figure out where you are!

Why Play?
- Improve your geography skills
- Explore new places from your screen
- Compete with friends for the best score
- Learn fun facts about different locations
